Pokemon Sword

This is Pokemon Sword.

Pokemon sword is a game where you follow a story line at first, exploring all of the different areas of the world, whilst catching Pokemon on the way.

Once you have finished the storyline, you can roam around the grounds of the world
to find the rest of the pokemon - collecting them all!

When you are catching them, they attack
but you can defend yourself and catch them in your pokeball.

I like this game because of the storyline and catching the pokemon, I find it interesting :D
