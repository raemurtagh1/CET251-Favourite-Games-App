Mario Kart


This is Mario Kart.

Mario kart is a game where you select a character, a vehicle and a course - you can then race around the course to win!

They are many different options when it comes to the selection aspect. There are many characters to choose from, different parts and types of the vehicle that you can change and 32 courses to choose from.

me, I have been playing the game pretty much since it has come out, and always
