Minecraft

This is Minecraft.

Minecraft is a game where you have a plethora of blocks to choose from in order
to create something within the world you have landed in!

There are 2 different modes - creative and survival.

In creative mode - you can create whatever you like (unlimited resources)

In survival mode - you have to collect resources and attack the zombies at night.

I like the game because I have played it for years and I like the creative aspect.
