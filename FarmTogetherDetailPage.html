Farm Together


This is Farm Together.

Farm together is a game where you can create your own farm - you can grow crops and trees, build buildings, ride around in
your tractor and look after animals.

As it states in the name, it is meant to be a multiplayer game where you and a friend
can create your own farm but it can be played on your own aswell.

I like the game because I like the creativitity aspect - you can create any type of farm
you want and have the placement of items be anywhere.
